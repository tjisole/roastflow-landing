Roast Profiles

Nail it once.
Repeat it forever.

When you pull off the perfect roast — the one where the crack hits right, the color is dead on, and the cup scores through the roof — save it as a profile. Every detail is captured: event timings, temperatures, gas and air adjustments, alarm targets. Next time you roast that coffee, load the profile and follow your own playbook.

💎
Save Your Best Work
Finish a roast you're proud of? One tap saves it as a named profile. The entire event timeline is captured — every observation, every machine adjustment, every temperature reading — exactly as it happened.
🎛️
Full Machine Blueprint
Profiles don't just save bean observations. They capture your exact gas settings, air damper positions, and the precise second you made each adjustment. Load a profile and your starting air, fuel mode, and gas level are pre-set.
⏱️
Guided Alarms
Every event in your saved profile becomes an alarm target. Time-based alerts ("open air at 4:30"), temperature-based alerts ("reduce gas when beans hit 380°F"), or both — your profile builds the alarm checklist automatically.
🔁
One-Click Load
Starting a new roast? Search your profile library by coffee name, roast level, or profile name. Select one and everything pre-fills — charge temp, weight, roast level, machine settings, and alarm targets. Ready to roast in seconds.
📊
Consistency Across Batches
Same coffee, same profile, same results. Track how closely each batch follows the blueprint. Compare timing tolerances across roasts and tighten your consistency batch after batch.
🎓
Built-In Training Tool
Hand a new roaster a profile and they have a complete step-by-step guide: when to adjust gas, when to open air, what temperature to watch for at each stage. Spoken voice alerts walk them through every move. Your expertise becomes their training manual — no shadowing required.
Built for the Real World

Internet drops.
Your roast doesn't.

Roasters don't work in server rooms. WiFi cuts out, connections drop, browsers crash — RoastFlow keeps working through all of it. Your data is never lost.

📡
Live Connection Monitor
RoastFlow constantly monitors your connection. If it drops, you'll see a clear banner instantly — no guessing whether your data is saving.
🛡️
Roast Session Protection
Mid-roast data is continuously saved to your device. If your browser crashes or WiFi drops, reopen the page and pick up exactly where you left off — timer, events, and all.
🔄
Automatic Sync
Finish a roast while offline? It's queued locally and syncs automatically when your connection returns. No manual steps, no lost data.
📋
Offline Logbook
Can't start a full session without internet? Log batch weights, roast levels, and notes in the offline logbook. Everything syncs when you're back online.
Business Operations

From green invoice
to roasted price.

Stop juggling spreadsheets. RoastFlow connects your green coffee invoices, inventory, production output, and pricing into one flow — so you know what you have, what it cost, and what to charge.

🤖
AI Invoice Import
Upload a PDF from your broker. Claude AI reads every line — coffee name, origin, weight, price per pound — and builds your purchase order in seconds. No manual data entry.
📦
Green Coffee Inventory
Receive coffee into inventory from your invoices. Track bags, weight, and cost per pound by origin. Move to production when you're ready to roast. Mark old lots as used with one click.
🏭
Production Tracking
Move coffee from inventory to the production queue. Track what's available, what's been roasted, and what's left. Multiple bags per roast? RoastFlow handles partial bag deductions automatically.
💰
Accurate Pricing
Green cost, overhead, labor — RoastFlow calculates your true cost per pound so you price with confidence. When costs change, your pricing updates with them.
👥
Team Management
Invite your team with one click — they get an email with a join link. Assign roles: roaster, warehouse, billing, viewer. Everyone sees only what they need.
📊
Reports & Export
Filter by day, week, month, or custom range. See total lbs roasted, average weight loss, revenue by origin. Export to CSV for your accountant.
Pricing & Profitability

Three channels.
One clear picture.

Retail, wholesale, and internet — each channel gets its own pricing strategy. RoastFlow breaks down your true cost per pound and shows you exactly what to charge, so every bag you sell makes money.

🏪
Channel-Specific Pricing
Set independent prices for retail, wholesale, and internet sales. Each channel tracks its own margin, markup, and version history. No more one-size-fits-all pricing across your business.
📐
True Cost Breakdown
See the full formula: green bean cost + shipping + labor + overhead = your true cost per pound. Green and shipping costs auto-fill from your latest purchase orders. No spreadsheet required.
🎯
Margin or Price — You Choose
Set a target margin and let RoastFlow calculate your price, or lock in your price and watch the margin calculate instantly. Color-coded alerts flag healthy margins in green, tight margins in amber, and below-cost pricing in red.
⚠️
Green Cost Alerts
When your broker's price changes, RoastFlow detects it automatically. A banner shows exactly how much your green cost shifted — up or down — so you can decide whether to adjust retail prices or absorb the change.
🧮
COGS Worksheet
Input your rent, utilities, insurance, equipment, and packaging costs. Add workers with hourly rates and monthly hours. RoastFlow divides it all by your production volume and calculates overhead and labor cost per pound — automatically.
📜
Version History & Comparison
Every pricing change is versioned. Compare cost-of-goods between versions with up/down indicators. See which coffees have changed cost and which channels need repricing — all at a glance.
